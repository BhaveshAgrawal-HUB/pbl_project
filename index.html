MediShield – Personalized Prediction of Medicine Side Effects

Hypothesis / Project Statement:
Can machine learning models analyze patient-specific medical data to predict the likelihood of adverse drug side effects before treatment begins, enabling preventive healthcare decisions?

Problem Statement

Thousands of patients experience unexpected side effects due to long-term medications. Current systems only list possible side effects but do not predict which patient is at risk before symptoms appear.

  • Delayed detection of adverse reactions
  • Improper dosage risks
  • Lack of personalized risk assessment
  • Reactive rather than preventive healthcare

Literature Review / Research

Recent research in AI-driven healthcare highlights the effectiveness of machine learning and deep learning techniques in predicting drug side effects using patient-specific datasets and adverse reaction databases.

Research Gap / Innovation

Existing systems provide static side-effect lists but lack a unified predictive framework. MediShield introduces an integrated ML pipeline for real-time personalized side-effect risk prediction.
